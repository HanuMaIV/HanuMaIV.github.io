By Brendan and Geovanna

What is pair programming?

Technique utilizing two programmers that work together in a programming environment.

One programmer is the 'driver' and the other is the 'navigator'.

Driver vs. Navigator

The driver writes code.

The navigator reads the lines of code and checks for proper syntax, or any errors.

Why is pair programming important at DBC?

DBC's goal is to prep their students to be world class developers.

Pair programming develops growth by enabling students to work in cooperative social environment.
