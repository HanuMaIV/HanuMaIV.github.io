About Me

Hello my name is Brendan Miranda. I am currently a student at DevBootcamp. I have always had a fascination with technology. At the age of 18 I graduated from Computer Education Institute (with honors) and obtained my Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer, Novell CNA, Comptia A+ and Comptia Network+ certifications. Many jobs followed that but I ultimately ended up as a System Administrator for a large casino. I enjoyed my time there. There was always something to learn.

Ultimately my life led me down other paths as I moved to Los Angeles to pursue music studies at Musician's Institute as a student of the BIT program (bass guitar). I still actively perform. I love playing cover songs of all genres.

The next chapter of my life came as I enrolled in a Yoga Teacher Training program. After graduating and obtaining my 200 RYT Yoga Alliance certification I am now teaching Yoga at Dan Henderson's Fitness center. I really enjoy teaching Yoga and have seen first hand the peace and transformation that Yoga has brought to myself and others.
